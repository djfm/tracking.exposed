Sun, Mar 1, 2020
  • In regards of facebook.tracking.exposed, it is available browser extension v2.0. New adopters get a splash screen on the first Facebook access, old adopters, should notice a logo upgrade. Surprisingly, despite we weren’t provide many updates on fbtrex, we see a constant curiosity from the internet (press on the months button to see wider). This work has been done by Alberto Granzotto, also as the first browser extension, and would help us in supporting more platforms.
